Explore Top Destinations in Perlis

Top Attractions in Perlis

Gua Kelam

Category: Nature

A limestone cave in Kaki Bukit, ideal for nature lovers and photography.

Open: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Ticket: RM2 (Adult), RM1 (Child)

Wang Kelian

Category: Shopping

Famous cross-border market at the Malaysia–Thailand border.

Open: Weekends only

Ticket: Free

Taman Ular dan Reptilia

Category: Family & Education

Educational reptile park for families and children.

Open: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Ticket: RM5 (Adult), RM2 (Child)

Tasik Melati

Category: Nature & Relaxation

Peaceful lake with walking tracks, pavilions and lotus flowers.

Open: 6:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Ticket: Free

Bukit Kubu

Category: History & Nature

Scenic hill with caves and beautiful views of Kangar.

Open: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Ticket: Free

Padang Besar

Category: Shopping & Culture

Popular for shopping, Thai cuisine, and cultural fusion.

Open: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Ticket: Free

Masjid Al-Hussain Kuala Perlis

Category: Religious & Architecture

Floating mosque built over the sea, a great sunset spot.

Open: 24 hours (non-Muslims outside prayer time)

Ticket: Free

Bukit Chabang

Category: Hiking & Adventure

Iconic twin peaks popular for hiking and panoramic photography.

Open: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Ticket: Free (own risk)

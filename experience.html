Experience Exploring Perlis


Why need to choose Perlis as your next experiences?

Perlis, the smallest state in Malaysia, offers stunning natural beauty, rich culture and mouth-watering northern cuisine. Its border with Thailand makes Perlis an ideal shopping and weekend getaway destination.


Attractions

Bukit Keteri

Climb limestone formations while enjoying the Perlis paddy field panorama.

Gua Kelam

Explore a 370m long limestone cave with a fascinating underground river.

Tasik Melati

Relax in a lotus-filled lake while riding an Instagram-worthy wooden platform.

Culture of Perlis

Explore authentic heritage, traditions and community lifestyles of Perlis.

Perlis Dialect

The local northern accent is friendly and distinctive. Example: hangpa (all of you), nak pi mana? (where are you going?).

Traditional Food

Laksa Perlis, pulut mempelam, pickled fish, dangai cake and karas reflect both Malay & Siamese influences.

Arts & Games

Wayang Kulit Gedek, dikir barat, silat drumming and traditional games such as Main Teri are still preserved today.

Festivals & Religion

Aidilfitri, Aidiladha, Prophet's Birthday are celebrated widely; the Thai community celebrates Songkran & Loy Krathong at local temples.

Architecture

Traditional wooden houses on stilts and landmarks like Masjid Alwi show a blend of Islamic and colonial influences.

Agriculture

Harvest season teamwork, village feasts, and rubber plantations highlight a strong community spirit in rural areas.
